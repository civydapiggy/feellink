Our mission

Many autistic children live in a very quiet world. They may not answer when you call, or show feelings in ways adults can easily read. FeelLink exists to offer one more concrete tool: play experiences that invite shared response between a child and the people who love them.

What we build

Our tools are intentionally minimal in form, but rich in the way they respond to touch, sound and shared play.

FeelLink Emotion Piano

A pixel piano that “wakes up” with the child — and shines brighter with a parent.

  • Basic lights and tones when the child plays alone.
  • Special smiles and richer feedback only when a caregiver joins.
  • Designed to practice shared attention and simple turn-taking.

Social Bridge Play Modes

Interaction logic that rewards togetherness, not just solo skills.

  • Solo mode: predictable responses to build a sense of safety.
  • Child + parent mode: extra emotional cues and animations.
  • Family mode: the fullest experience when more people join.

Why your donation matters

Donations help us prototype, test and distribute low-cost emotional learning toys to autistic children and their families. We focus on:

  • Subsidising pilot kits for families who cannot afford new tools.
  • Working with therapists and educators to refine play patterns.
  • Sharing our learnings openly so other makers and NGOs can build on them.

About FeelLink

Who we are

FeelLink is a small design-driven initiative exploring how physical toys and simple interactions can support autistic children and their caregivers. We collaborate with families, therapists and educators to keep our work grounded in real life.

How to connect

If you are a parent, therapist, school or NGO interested in piloting our tools or co-creating new ones, we would love to hear from you.
