Our approach

We follow a responsible and science-guided development process:

  • We begin with research to understand the sensory, communication and developmental needs of autistic children.
  • We design, prototype and test interactive toys that are safe, predictable and easy to use.
  • We consult with doctors, therapists, clinical institutions and special-needs educators to refine every design.
  • After professional validation, we raise funding to manufacture the toys at scale.
  • Through partner charities, we donate these toys to families in need and gather feedback to improve further.

What we build

We design interactive toys that respond gently, predictably and meaningfully — helping autistic children practice emotional understanding, communication and shared attention.

FeelLink Emotion Piano

A pixel-based musical toy that responds differently to solo play and shared play.

  • Gentle sounds and predictable lights during solo use, supporting a sense of safety.
  • Enhanced expressions and richer feedback when a caregiver joins.
  • Useful in daily routines and therapy sessions.

Social-Bridge Play Modes

Interaction logic that rewards cooperation rather than only individual performance.

  • Solo mode — low sensory load and consistent responses.
  • Child + adult mode — emotional cues, smiles and supportive animations.
  • Group mode — fullest patterns when several people play together.

About FeelLink

Who we are

FeelLink is a design-driven non-profit team focused on creating safe, science-grounded interactive toys for autistic children. We collaborate with families, therapists, educators and clinical professionals to ensure our work stays respectful, practical and grounded in real needs.

Our permanent non-profit commitment:
FeelLink is, and will always remain, a non-profit initiative. We do not sell our toys. Every donation and every hour of work goes solely to creating safe, science-based tools for autistic children and giving them away for free.
